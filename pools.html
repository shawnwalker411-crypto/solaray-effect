üåé Why Geography Matters

Lower latency = fewer stale shares = more income. Pools with servers in North America will give you fewer rejected shares if you're mining from the US or Canada. Most major pools have global server networks, but distance to the nearest server still matters.

Smart Switching Explained:

AntPool and ViaBTC let you mine BTC or BCH using the same stratum URL. You switch which coin from the pool's website with one click — no logging into your miner, no changing settings. ViaBTC also has an auto mode that switches for you based on real-time profitability.

This is NOT merged mining. You mine one coin at a time. You're just able to switch without touching your hardware.
