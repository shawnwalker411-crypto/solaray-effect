North America Mining Pools

Pools with USA/Canada stratum servers for lower latency

Why Use a North America Pool?

Lower latency = fewer stale shares. When your miner submits work, milliseconds matter. A server in Virginia vs one in China can be the difference between accepted and rejected shares. For home miners on standard internet, aim for pools with servers in the USA or Canada.
