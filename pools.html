Why Geography Matters

Lower latency = fewer stale shares. Connect to pools with servers near you. From the USA, aim for North America pools first. Worldwide pools with NA servers are also good options.

Payout Methods Explained

FPPS (Full Pay Per Share)

Paid for every valid share plus estimated transaction fees. Most predictable income.

PPS (Pay Per Share)

Paid for every share submitted. No transaction fee bonus.

PPS+

Block rewards via PPS, transaction fees via PPLNS. Hybrid approach.

PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares)

Paid when pool finds block, based on recent shares. More variance.

TIDES

OCEAN's transparent accounting system for decentralized mining.

Solo

Only paid if your miner finds a block. Lottery mining - high risk, high reward.
